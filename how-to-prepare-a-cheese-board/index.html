How to Prepare a Cheese Board

Creating a cheese board is a delightful way to entertain guests or enjoy a variety of cheeses and accompaniments. Start by selecting a variety of cheeses with different textures and flavors, such as soft, creamy brie, sharp cheddar, and tangy blue cheese. Arrange the cheeses on a large serving platter or wooden board, leaving space for accompaniments. Add crackers, bread, and sliced baguette for serving, as well as a variety of fruits, nuts, olives, and charcuterie. Garnish with fresh herbs and honey or jam for drizzling. Serve at room temperature and encourage guests to mix and match flavors and textures.
